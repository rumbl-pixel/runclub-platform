# Gwynne Park Run Club Design Direction

## Obsidian Glass

The app should feel like a polished navy glass school sport dashboard: deep obsidian navy foundations, translucent glass panels, crisp white trim, and strong readable content surfaces.

Reference direction: https://styles.refero.design/style/d271a6c4-942f-4abf-a3de-66795f15f031

## Visual Rules

- Use obsidian navy as the main atmosphere, especially headers, hero areas, kiosk surfaces, and page depth.
- Use white trim for borders, panel edges, focus rings, and control outlines.
- Use glass surfaces with blur, subtle translucency, and restrained shadows.
- Keep cards and data-heavy areas readable with light glass surfaces over the navy background.
- Use rounded pills for top-level navigation and primary controls.
- Keep motion calm: smooth scrolling, slight hover lift, and no distracting decorative effects.
- Avoid leaning on school gold for this pass; the look should be navy glass plus white trim.

## Interaction Feel

Scrolling should feel app-like and smooth across iPad, phone, and laptop. Controls should stay stable, tables should keep readable widths in horizontal scroll containers, and hover/focus states should make the interface feel precise rather than flashy.
